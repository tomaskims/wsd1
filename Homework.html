Delicious Tacos!!

You will love them tacos

Taken from my aunt's recipe book

So delicious that you would think they were brought straight from Mexico

Ingredients:

Make sure you use fresh ingredients

  • 2lb pork.
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Fresh corn
  • Green sauce (salsa verde)
  • Corn tortillas
  • Green pepper
  • Jalapenos

Directions

  1. Cook pork for about an hour until the meat is soft. serve with the jalapenos and corn and pour the green sauce over it.
  2. mix the green peppers and tomatoes to fill the tortillas.
